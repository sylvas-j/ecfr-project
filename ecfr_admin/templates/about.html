Electronic Course Form Registration

ECFR is design to manage student course registration

The system has three privileges

  • Admin(exam and record): this is responsible for approving student registered courses.
    And adding and managing courses on the system.
  • HOD: this is responsible for choosing whom will serve as an admin.
    And it also have a read-only access to the admin activities.
  • Students: this enable students to submit courses for approval.
    And check registered courses.
